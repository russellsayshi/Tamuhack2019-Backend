Carma
Seamless intercar communication
Carma provides a seamless end-to-end and mobile-first way to notify other drivers of immediate problems with their car, through crowdsourcing.

How it works

"Ok google, open carma"
  -  Integrates seamlessly with Google maps navigation
"Tell the green volvo that its tail light is out"
  -  Carma utilizes the Smartcar API to detect your present location and fetch a list of nearby Smartcar users
The other car receives a push notification with the information
  -  The app closes automatically and returns you to your navigation
